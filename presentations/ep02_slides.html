Solution: BPE

Byte Pair Encoding

Idea: Jo characters BAAR BAAR saath aate hain,
unhe merge kar do!

BPE: Step by Step

Step 1: m + e → me (found 3x)
Step 2: me + r → mer (found 3x)
Step 3: mer + a → mera (found 3x)
Step 4: a + a → aa (found 3x)

Ab "mera" = 4 characters lekin 1 token!
